## 📖 The Concept
**Inflow Protocol** is a decentralized invoice factoring engine built on EVM. It tokenizes real-world B2B invoices into non-fungible tokens (NFTs) and leverages decentralized USDC liquidity pools (Vaults) to provide instant cash advances to freelancers and SMEs. 

Instead of relying on speculative tokenomics, Inflow Protocol generates **Real-World Yield** driven by actual business activities and corporate debt settlement.

## ⚠️ The Problem
The current financial system is broken for independent workers and small businesses:
1. **The Cash Flow Death Valley:** Freelancers and agencies often face 30, 60, or even 90-day payment terms (Net-30/60/90) from corporate clients. This kills their cash flow and stunts business growth.
2. **High Barriers to Traditional Finance:** Traditional banks and factoring companies require massive minimum volumes, endless paperwork, and charge predatory hidden fees, making them inaccessible for modern gig workers.

## 💡 The Solution
Inflow Protocol bridges the gap between TradFi and Web3 by providing:
- **Instant Cash Advance:** Get paid immediately for your completed work. Up to 80% of the invoice value is advanced upfront.
- **Trustless Execution:** Smart contracts automatically handle origination fees, yield splits, and reserve payouts without human intervention or commingling of funds.
- **Institutional-Grade Accounting:** Strict separation between operational wallets and treasury wallets ensures clean P&L and transparent on-chain auditing.

## 🎯 Target Market
Our dual-sided marketplace serves two distinct groups:
1. **The Borrowers (Freelancers, Agencies, & SMEs):** Professionals who need instant liquidity to pay rent, scale their business, or cover operational costs without waiting for slow corporate clients.
2. **The Lenders (DeFi Liquidity Providers):** Web3 investors looking for stable, predictable, and sustainable yield backed by legally binding real-world corporate debt, isolated from crypto market volatility.

## 🏗️ Architecture & Network Plan
Inflow Protocol is engineered to be highly scalable, secure, and gas-efficient.
- **Smart Contracts:** Built with Solidity and Hardhat.
- **Network:** Targeted for deployment on **Base Mainnet** (Layer 2). We chose Base for its lightning-fast finality, near-zero transaction fees, and direct bridge to institutional liquidity via the Coinbase ecosystem.
- **Frontend / DApp:** Built with Next.js, React, and Web3 integration tools (ethers.js / wagmi).
